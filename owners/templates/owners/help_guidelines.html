Help & Guidelines

1. How to Post Your Daily Menu

To post your daily menu, navigate to the "Post Your Daily Menu" option on the dashboard. You can enter breakfast, lunch, and/or dinner menus. You are free to upload the menu for one, two, or all three meals at once. Each menu item should include a name, description, and price.

2. How to Edit or Delete a Menu

If you need to edit or delete a posted menu, go to the "Manage Menu" section from your dashboard. There you will see options to edit or remove your menus for any meal (breakfast, lunch, or dinner).

3. How to View Your Menu

You can view your posted menus by clicking on the "View Your Menu" option in your dashboard. The menus will be displayed with meal times and details for each item youâ€™ve posted.

4. Managing Special Offers & Promotions

To create or view special offers, go to the "Special Offers & Promotions" section. Here you can add a title, details, and an expiry date for the offer, which will be displayed to customers.

5. Viewing Ratings & Feedback

You can view the ratings and feedback provided by customers under the "Ratings" section in your dashboard. This helps you get insights into how customers perceive your service and improve accordingly.

6. Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, head over to the "Contact Support" section, where you can reach out to our team for help.
